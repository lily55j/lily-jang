Profile

Born in South Korea, Lily Jang is a self-taught artist/illustrator, now based in Tokyo, Japan.
She began creating art at the age of 5 under the influence of her mother who are an artist.
After being raised in the paint and the art scene, with years of experimenting in different countries,
she rediscovered her love for fine art and illustration.
In 2010, Lily moved to Tokyo, and in 2011 re-entered the art world.
She began creating illustration and art under the influence of city life; Tokyo life.
